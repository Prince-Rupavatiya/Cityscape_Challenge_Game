About the cityscape Challenges game


Cityscape Challenges is a 2D game having a virtual city with a variety of
challenges gives the player thechance to navigate through various places
in the city while overcoming the obstacles. The game is developed by:

  Aatreyee Joshi

  Bharvy Savaliya

  Riya Shah

  Tirth Patel

  Prince Rupavatiya

 How to Play:


1) Player:

 - Movement: Use the following controls for player movement on the map:

 - Up Arrow Key or W: Move upwards

 - Down Arrow Key or S: Move downwards

 - Left Arrow Key or A: Move to the left

 - Right Arrow Key or D: Move to the right


2) Map:

 - City Map: Press the map icon on your right to view the city map.

 - Navigation: Move onto places to explore them.

 - Exit: Press enter to exit the map.


3) Energy and Life:

 - Energy: Be cautious, each accident reduces your energy.

 - Lives: You have a maximum of three lives. Beware of police cars; getting caught costs a life.

 - Energy Maintenance: Replenish energy by returning home.
